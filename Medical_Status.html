Healthcare Infrastructure

Government and private institutions shaping the city's health landscape.

Government & Public Institutions

  • Government General Hospital (GGH): The primary public hospital, serving a large population with general and specialized care.
  • Rajahmundry Medical College: A key institution for medical education and research, with a teaching hospital.

Private & Super-Specialty Hospitals

  • Apollo Hospitals: A multi-specialty hospital providing advanced care in cardiology, oncology, and more.
  • Care Hospitals: Known for its specialized services and modern facilities.
  • Medicover Hospitals: Offers comprehensive healthcare services, including critical care.

Specialty Care Centers

  • Vasan Eye Care & L.V. Prasad Eye Institute: Leading centers for ophthalmology, serving the entire region.
  • Rainbow Children's Hospital: A dedicated institution for pediatric and maternal care.
  • Sri Sathya Sai Orthopaedic & Trauma Care: A major center for orthopedic and trauma-related services.

Disease Burden & Trends

A snapshot of prevalent health challenges in the city.

Chronic Diseases

High prevalence of Diabetes, Hypertension, and Cardiovascular Diseases due to lifestyle changes.

Infectious Diseases

Notable cases of Dengue, Malaria, and seasonal viral fevers, often linked to sanitation and urban development.

Nutritional & Maternal Health

Prevalence of anemia and malnutrition, especially among women and children in lower-income areas. High maternal and infant care demand.

Mental Health

Increasing recognition of mental health issues, with a growing need for counseling and support services for stress, anxiety, and depression.

WHO Reports & Case List

Data from public health reports and case tracking.

Report on Dengue Outbreaks (2022)

WHO report highlighted a significant spike in Dengue cases due to post-monsoon water stagnation. Recommendations included improved vector control and community awareness campaigns.

  • Case #2022-001: A 35-year-old male with severe symptoms.
  • Case #2022-002: A 12-year-old child from a low-lying area.
  • Case #2022-003: A 50-year-old female with comorbidities.

Report on Malnutrition (2020-2021)

A joint report on nutritional deficiencies indicated a high prevalence of anemia among adolescent girls and Vitamin D deficiency in urban children. The report suggested fortifying staple foods and educational programs.

  • Case #2021-015: A 16-year-old with chronic iron-deficiency anemia.
  • Case #2021-020: A 7-year-old with signs of stunted growth.

Analysis & Insights

Key public health challenges and areas for improvement.

Public Health Challenges

  • Sanitation & Waste Management: Directly impacts infectious disease spread.
  • Access to Care: Disparities exist between urban centers and surrounding areas.
  • Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs): A rising burden due to changing lifestyles.

Areas for Improvement

  • Preventive Care & Health Education: Focus on lifestyle management and early detection.
  • Digital Health Integration: Utilizing telemedicine and EHRs for better data management.
  • Mental Health Services: Expanding access to professional psychological support.
