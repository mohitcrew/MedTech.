About Us

MedTech Rajahmundry – Bridging Technology and Healthcare in the Cultural Capital

Welcome to MedTech Rajahmundry, a dedicated platform showcasing the evolution of healthcare and bringing vital medical information closer to the people of our city. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and healthcare, empowering citizens, healthcare workers, and researchers with reliable insights into Rajahmundry’s health infrastructure.

Our Vision

To establish Rajahmundry as a model smart-health city, where every citizen can easily access healthcare data, hospitals can connect seamlessly with patients, and technology fosters healthier lives for all.
